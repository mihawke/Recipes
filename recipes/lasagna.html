Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish known for its rich layers of flavor and hearty, comforting appeal. It traditionally consists of wide, flat pasta sheets layered with a variety of fillings, which often include a savory blend of ground meats, such as beef or sausage, a tomato-based sauce, and creamy b√©chamel or ricotta cheese. These layers are typically seasoned with garlic, onions, and a medley of Italian herbs like basil and oregano, creating a complex, aromatic profile. Once assembled, the lasagna is baked until bubbling and golden, with a deliciously crisp top layer of melted cheese. Versatile and customizable, lasagna can be adapted to include vegetables, different cheeses, or even alternative protein sources, making it a versatile favorite for family dinners, special occasions, and gatherings.

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
