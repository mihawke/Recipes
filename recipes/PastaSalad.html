Easy Asian Pasta Salad

Description:

This easy Asian pasta salad is a delightful fusion of flavors and textures, perfect as a light meal or a side dish for any occasion. The optional vegetables add a fresh crunch, making it a versatile and refreshing addition to your recipe collection.

Ingredients:

  • 1 (8 ounce) package spaghetti
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 6 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Â¼ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds
  • 2 teaspoons sweet chili sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped (Optional)
  • 1 cup sugar snap peas (Optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the Spaghetti: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain the pasta and rinse with cold water to cool it down. Toss with 1 teaspoon of olive oil to prevent sticking.
  2. Prepare the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the soy sauce, white sugar, rice vinegar, toasted sesame seeds, sweet chili sauce, and sesame oil until the sugar is dissolved and the mixture is well combined.
  3. Combine Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked spaghetti, chopped green onions, red bell pepper, and sugar snap peas (if using).
  4. Dress the Salad: Pour the dressing over the pasta and vegetables. Toss everything together until the pasta is evenly coated with the dressing.
  5. Serve and Enjoy: Chill the salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld. Serve cold or at room temperature.
